Rapid River Rafting

The purpose and mission of Rapid River Rafting is for you to enjoy your time while being safe! We work hard and play even harder!

History

Rapid River Rafting started in 2020, right when the world was dealing with COVID-19. It was a tough time for everyone, and people were looking for safe ways to have fun and enjoy life. That is where we came in! We wanted to help folks experience the joy of being out on the water, especially when so many other activities were off-limits.

Our river guides are super friendly and experienced—some even used to be professional kayakers! They love sharing their passion for rafting with everyone, whether you’re a pro or just want to give it a try. We offer different trips throughout the year, so there’s always a chance to jump on a raft and have a blast.
